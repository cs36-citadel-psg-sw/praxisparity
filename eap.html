Technical Foundations

Foundation 01

Ontological Mapping

We move beyond simple 'key-value' pairs. The platform builds a semantic graph of every entity in your stack—from a single container to a global load balancer. By understanding the *intent* behind the infrastructure, we can identify when the system is behaving technically correctly but epistemically incorrectly.

Foundation 02

Heuristic Auditing

Every automated action taken by the platform is logged with an 'Epistemic Weight.' This audit trail shows exactly which data points were considered and which were discarded, mitigating the 'Black Box' problem common in traditional AI operations (AIOps).

Foundation 03

Temporal Consensus

Truth is time-bound. Our reconciliation engine uses vector clocks and causal ordering to ensure that the "truth" we report accounts for latency and eventual consistency across distributed systems.
